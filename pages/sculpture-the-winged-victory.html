The Winged Victory of Samothrace

Artist: Ancient Greek

4.8/5

"The Winged Victory of Samothrace" is an ancient Greek marble sculpture depicting Nike, the goddess of victory. Created around 190 BCE, it is celebrated for its dramatic movement and exquisite detailing, symbolizing triumph and power.

Year: c. 190 BCE

Medium: Marble

Dimensions: 244 cm (8 ft)

Location: Louvre Museum, Paris

Price: Priceless (Cultural Heritage)
