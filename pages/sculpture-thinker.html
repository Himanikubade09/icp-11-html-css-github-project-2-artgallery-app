The Thinker

Artist:Auguste Rodin

4.7/5

"The Thinker" is a bronze sculpture by Auguste Rodin, originally conceived in 1880 as part of The Gates of Hell. It depicts a man in deep thought, symbolizing philosophy and intellectual struggle. The statue has been cast in multiple sizes and versions worldwide.


Year:1904 (first full-scale cast)

Medium: Bronze

Dimensions: 181 cm (71.5 in)

Location: Rodin Museum, Paris (multiple casts exist worldwide)

Price: $1150 Million
