The Creation of Adam

Artist: Michelangelo

4.7/5

"The Creation of Adam" is a renowned fresco painted by Michelangelo between 1511 and 1512 on the ceiling of the Sistine Chapel in Vatican City. The artwork depicts God giving life to Adam through an outstretched hand, symbolizing divine connection and human potential. It is one of the most celebrated images in the history of art.


Year:1511–1512

Medium: Fresco

Dimensions: 280 cm × 570 cm (110 in × 224 in)

Location: Sistine Chapel, Vatican City

Price: $150 Billion
