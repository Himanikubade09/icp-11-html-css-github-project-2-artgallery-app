David

Artist: Michelangelo

4.7/5

"David" is one of the most renowned sculptures of the Renaissance period, created by Michelangelo between 1501 and 1504. This marble statue represents the biblical hero David before his battle with Goliath. Standing at 17 feet tall, the sculpture showcases Michelangelo’s mastery of anatomy and detail.


Year:1501–1504

Medium: Marble

Dimensions: 517 cm (17 ft)

Location: Galleria dell'Accademia, Florence
