The Kiss

Artist: Gustav Klimt

5/5

"The Kiss" is an iconic painting by Gustav Klimt, created during his Golden Phase. The artwork features a couple wrapped in a golden embrace, symbolizing love, passion, and intimacy. The intricate patterns and use of gold leaf give the painting a luminous quality.

Year: 1907–1908

Medium: Oil and Gold Leaf on Canvas

Dimensions: 180 cm × 180 cm (71 in × 71 in)

Location: Österreichische Galerie Belvedere, Vienna

Price: $250 Million
